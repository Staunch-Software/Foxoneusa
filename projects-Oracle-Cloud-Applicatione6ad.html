    Sensing the demand at the appropriate time is one of the biggest challenges that supply chain executives face regularly. But after the introduction of the Oracle Demand Planning Cloud solution, executives can accurately forecast customer demands, plan for the supply chain, and deliver on time. Applying cognitive intelligence for forecasting new products, segmenting customers and products for better accuracy, attach-rate-based forecasting, and ease of forecast tuning for end-users are some of the aspects that have given Oracle Demand Planning Cloud a competitive edge over other products.
    This solution can help you align your plans and operations with S&OP and integrated business planning. Oracle Demand Planning Cloud assists supply chain executives to monitor operations in real-time and help make smarter decisions for the better good of the company.

    Oracle Supply Planning is a cloud-based planning solution that helps companies Plan, Monitor and Respond to changes in the supply chain process. It allows users to Visualize Planning Problems, Investigate Root Causes and get Real-Time Feedback as you update or modify your plan. Maximize the utilization of your resources, optimize warehouse operations, and define the best output for your supply chain processes.
    Oracle Supply Planning Cloud is a constraint-enabled planning engine that provides an optimal supply chain planning solution considering lead time resource and supplier capacity constraints in the supply chain network. It has a powerful user interface to allow planners to perform root causes and real-time simulation and release orders to the execution system. Stochastic safety stock planning, plan comparison, plan archival, custom exceptions, and custom measures are some of the state-of-the-art capabilities that businesses have been able to capitalize on to transform their supply chain planning process into a digital platform.

    Traditionally, Sales and Operations Planning (S&OP), is a process of matching a manufacturing organization’s supply with demand by having the sales department collaborate with operations department to deliver an actionable and executable production plan based on the agreed-upon consensus forecast. With Oracle Cloud Sales and Operations Planning, one can achieve the true potential of S&OP and overall business objectives of the organization. S&OP is all about identifying performance gaps and closing those gaps, but only a handful of best-in-class companies have the ability to identify the gaps and perform a detailed root-cause analysis, making it impossible to effectively address those performance gaps. With Oracle S&OP Cloud, organizations not only balance their supply and demand but also monitor the health of product portfolio (pre-launch, post-launch and growth items), ensuring achieving revenue targets, generating a supply plan to profitably meet demand, and reconciling your operating plans with overall financial goals.

    Oracle Cloud Inventory Management is a cloud-based solution that enables businesses to manage their inventory effectively. This solution lets you gain real-time visibility into your inventory levels, track goods movements and streamline your supply chain management.
    The solution reduces inventory carrying costs, eliminates stockouts, and optimizes order fulfillment processes. Cloud Inventory Management is designed and developed to enhance your business’s efficiency, reduce operational costs, and improve customer satisfaction.

    Oracle Inventory Management has many features and functionality, making it a powerful solution for businesses. These features are among the most essential.
    Real-time inventory visibility
    With Cloud Inventory Management, you can simultaneously monitor your inventory at multiple locations. With this feature, you can track inventory movements, identify slow-moving items, and manage your inventory levels effectively.
    Multi-Channel Inventory Management
    You can manage your inventory across multiple channels using Oracle Inventory Management. With this feature, you can minimize stockouts, avoid overselling, and improve your order fulfillment process.
    Advanced Analytics and Reporting
    Inventory Management provides advanced analytics and reporting capabilities that allow you to analyze your inventory data, identify trends, and make informed decisions. Using this solution, you can create custom reports, track key performance indicators (KPIs), and monitor your inventory performance in real time.
    Order Management
    Order management is enabled by Oracle Cloud Inventory Management, which allows you to manage your orders more efficiently. As a result of this feature, you can track order status, address changes, and streamline your order fulfillment process.
    Mobile App Support
    With Cloud Inventory Management, you can manage your inventory operations wherever you go. This feature lets you view your inventory data, monitor inventory levels, and manage your orders from your mobile device.
